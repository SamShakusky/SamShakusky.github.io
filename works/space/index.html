Who We Are

Space Beyond is a transnational organization, which unite scientists and engineers from all over the world to make space travel safe, swift and accessible for every person on Earth. Light years are no more a big deal, since in 2021 the Russian physicist Dimtry Ermolenko discovered a way to build a warp-drive. After the years of thorough studies, the space travel beyond our solar system became a reality. And now, as the Alpha Centauri is already colonized, you have a unique opportunity to leave the Earth and find a new better home.
